WHAT IS KIDS WITH CAPES?

Kids With Capes is an NGO by children for children — where every child is a superhero! We inspire kindness, empathy, and leadership in young hearts, helping them believe that they can create real change in the world.

THE STORY BEHIND THE FOUNDATION

Founded by Neelima Latur and Dimple Sayani, the movement started when children began showing compassion towards others. What started as simple acts of kindness soon turned into a nationwide initiative inspiring thousands.

WHY IS KIDS WITH CAPES IMPORTANT?

  • Empowering Society: Encourages children to believe in their power to make change.
  • Self-Belief: Builds confidence and a growth mindset.
  • Personal Growth: Fosters leadership, empathy, and teamwork.
  • Recognition: Celebrates kindness and social responsibility.

Kids With Capes Kindness Deeds

Inspiring stories of compassion, courage, and leadership — where every small act creates a ripple of change

01

Uniform Drive for 25 Children

Observing children without uniforms at Sant Ghadge Maharaj School, Ronit decided to do something. On his birthday, he asked for donations instead of gifts, raising enough to sponsor uniforms for 25 kids.

02

Feeding and Playing with 40 Kids

Joining the Sri Sathya Sai Seva Organisation, kids made snacks and games for a ZP school in Lohgaon. They shared food, laughter, and kindness with 40 children — giving love the sweetest form.

03

Teddy Bear Day Celebration

Collaborating with professionals, kids hosted a “Teddy Bear Day” at Jesus Christ School. Ronit and Adaah managed departments for games, crafts, and food — brightening the day of 30 happy kids.

04

Blanket Donation for Watchmen

Continuing a family tradition, Ronit and Adaah saved their pocket money to buy blankets. Along with friends, they donated 20 blankets to watchmen who keep us safe in the cold.

05

Animal Awareness at the Gaushala

Ronit and Adaah led a visit to a gaushala, teaching others the importance of kindness toward animals. They inspired everyone to care for creatures big and small with love and respect.

KIDS WITH CAPES CLUB

WomanTV spotlighted Adaah’s kindness-driven efforts, inspiring kids to become superheroes of kindness! The Kids With Capes Club was created to promote leadership, compassion, and responsibility through meaningful acts of kindness.

Run with Feet-a-thon
