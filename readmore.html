Month of Execution: June 2024 onwards

Venue: Jehangir Hospital

Beneficiaries: Underprivileged diabetic kids

The "Sweetlings" program is a comprehensive rehabilitation initiative for children with Type 1 Diabetes. It brings together a team of pediatricians, pediatric endocrinologists, dietitians, diabetes educators, psychologists, social workers, and peer educators, with volunteers playing a regular role in the clinic.

Every Saturday, the clinic offers free services to underprivileged children at Jehangir Hospital, benefiting over 650 young patients. The project is supported by Reliance Foundation and aims to bring holistic care, guidance, and empowerment to children and families affected by diabetes.
